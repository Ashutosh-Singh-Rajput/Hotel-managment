DINNING

Dinning

The Hotel King offers a range of eclectic choices for dining throughout the day…

SHIMA

SOUTHEAST ASIAN

Shima is located on the top floor of The Island House. Sophisticated and relaxed open-air dining featuring the fresh, vibrant flavors of Southeast Asian cuisine.

WINES

WORLD RENOWN WINES

The Hotel King is renown for its vast selection of revered wines. Not only does each restaurant have an extensive collection hand-picked to pair with the unique culinary dishes they serve, but The Market is open 7 days-a-week, providing a space where wine can be tasted and purchased..

MOGANO BY GIORGIO LOCATELLI

ITALIAN

Mogano by Giorgio Locatelli brings the best of Italian cuisine to life in The Bahamas, with a range of exquisite pasta dishes and seasonal mains, as well as a distinctive wine list.

YELLOWBELL

CRAFT COCKTAIL BAR I BREAKFAST & LUNCH I COFFEE

Discover an extensive selection of all-new bespoke cocktails, as well as a beautifully curated bar bites menu from Chef Dave of Shima. In partnership with Bon Vivants.
